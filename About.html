Innovation with a history

Star Bicycle is pedaling with Cannondale

Hardly any other bike brand has set as many trends in the last 40 years as Cannondale. Even today, the US brand impresses with an innovative and wide range. That's why we decided to start the 2021 season with Cannondale in the Star Bicycle Shop.

Whether mountain bike, racing bike, gravel bike, electric bike, city bike, e-mountain bike or children's bike: Cannondale is at the forefront in all bike categories. As different as the different bikes are, they all have in common that they are exceptionally light, beautiful and well thought out.

These three features have been in the brand's DNA since the first models. As early as the early 1980s, Cannondale consistently built frames out of aluminum, when most of the major manufacturers only used steel. This made Cannondale bikes not only lighter, but also more stable than the competition. When this followed suit, Cannondale was already working with carbon.

Success also came because the Cannondale bikes looked incredibly good from the start. Older mountain bikers and racing cyclists still remember the almost invisible weld seams and finely polished pipe transitions. Cannondale cultivated this optical quality long before it gained a majority thanks to carbon.

When it comes to equipment, too, Cannondale wanted to offer its customers the best. If this didn't exist, the brand quickly invented it itself. This is how legendary parts such as the sensitive and stable Headshok suspension fork, the strong, easily adjustable Force40 brakes or the light and stiff bottom bracket standard PF30 were created. Cannondale didn't shy away from any. Expenditure. She also dared to go new, unfamiliar ways. The brand still does that today. Examples of this are the first gravel bikes built in large series or the unique suspension forks with in-house technology for these.

All of this made us want to work with Cannondale. We have found each other because we share with the brand the joy of technical perfection from which racing drivers benefit as much as hobby athletes and bicycle commuters. From the 2021 season, Cannondale bikes will round off our range. Various racing bikes, gravel bikes, electric bikes, mountain bikes, urban bikes and children's bikes have already arrived. You can find these presented here on the website, and you can now view, try out and buy them in our shop in Winterthur!
